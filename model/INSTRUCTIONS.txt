To execute:

1) Download files from the main model folder
2) Open OpenModelica (or your equivalent program) and load the PhysicsBasedPredictionRULv101 library.
3) Open IMECE2025v102.  Edit the filepath in batParams.SOCOCV to reflect the appropriate location of 30Q_SOCOCV.csv on your machine.
4) Edit parameters to suit and execute.  Recommended timestep 1 sec.
